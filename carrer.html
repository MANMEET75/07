Title:

Full Stack Developer with 4 years of experience

Location:

Remote

Job Description:

We are looking for a Full Stack Developer to join our team. candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications that are critical to our business operations.

Responsibilities:
  • Design and develop web-based applications from scratch to production
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and solve complex business problems.
  • Build responsive and user-friendly UIs using modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Develop APIs and integrate them with the front end to enable seamless data exchange.
  • Debug and resolve issues reported by users and identify the root cause of problems.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in Full Stack development.
  • Strong proficiency in front-end web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with modern front-end frameworks such as React or Angular.
  • Solid knowledge of server-side programming languages such as .NET .
  • Experience with backend frameworks such as Spring, Flask.
  • Knowledge of databases and SQL, with experience in designing and developing database schema.
